The new Iphone is here!

Apple's consumer-focused flagship models, the iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus , start at $799.
The professional-oriented iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone Pro Max,
which start at $999 and have more features and capabilities, are added to the group.The iPhone 15 family consists of a total of four models,
including two 6.1-inch iPhones and two 6.7-inch iPhones, similar to the iPhone 14 lineup from the previous year.
The 6.1-inch iPhone 15 and the 6.7-inch iPhone 15 Plus are available to consumers. The priciest, top-tier variants are the 6.1-inch iPhone 15 Pro and the 6.7-inch iPhone 15 Pro Max.
