THE NEW IPHONE 15

The wait is finally over – Apple has officially unveiled the brand-new iPhone 15 lineup! This highly anticipated launch brings exciting upgrades like the Dynamic Island, Always-On display, new 48MP camera, and exclusive pro features on the iPhone 15 Pro models. Apple launched the new iPhone 15 series on September 12, 2023, including iPhone 15, iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone 15 Pro, and iPhone 15 Pro Max. The Pro models feature an aerospace-grade titanium design, customized Action button, upgraded 48MP camera system, A17 Bionic chip, and more pro features like 8K ProRes video recording. The iPhone 15 and 15 Plus stick with the A16 chip from the iPhone 14 Pro but gain exciting new upgrades like the Dynamic Island, and improved battery life.
