Is Iphone 15 worth buying?

Apple once again proved that technology works and grows faster than ever. Recently they have launched the new Iphone 15, Iphone 15 Plus, Iphone 15 Pro, and the Iphone 15 Pro Max that has a lot of features that Apple users will surely like. These new released series of Iphone features an industry-first color-infused back glass with stunning, textured matte finish, and a new contoured edge on the aluminum enclosure. Both variants come equipped with the Dynamic Island and a cutting-edge camera system that enables users to capture wonderful images of ordinary events in their life. In this case, purchasing an Iphone 15 might be a great decision. Because who would not want to have a great camera to capture all memorable moments?
Furthermore, besides the camera, there are also other specifications that would surely make an Apple user buy this new released series immediately. Though all series are both newly released by Apple, it still has differences that makes every variant more unique to each other. Listed below are the specifications of each Iphone 15 variants.
