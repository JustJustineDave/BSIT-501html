Apple releases an iPhone 15 series

September 18, 2023


The iPhone 15 series was officially announced by Apple on September 12, 2023. The announcement event, called the "Wonderlust" event,
unveiled several models in the series including:

  1. iPhone 15
  2. iPhone 15 Plus
  3. iPhone 15 Pro
  4. iPhone 15 Pro Max
for more information about the event click here: Wonderlust event

These new iPhone models came with various improvements and features over their predecessors, including camera innovations, better battery life,
improved performance, and updated designs.

The iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus has five color options:
  • pink
  • yellow
  • green
  • blue
  • black
The storage capacities of iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus are as follows:
  • 128GB
  • 256GB
  • 512GB
On the other hand, the iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max are offered in colors:
  • black titanium
  • white titanium
  • blue titanium
  • natural titanium
Also the iPhone 15 Pro gets an additional 1TB storage option, while the iPhone 15 Pro Max comes storage capacities in:
  • 256GB
  • 512GB
  • 1TB

Following the announcement, pre-orders for the iPhone 15 models started on September 15, 2023, and the devices became available for purchase
and shipping on September 22, 2023 for over 40 countries including the US, the UK, India, Germany, Japan, and China.
