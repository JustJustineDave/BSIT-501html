The All New iPhone15

The all new iPhone15 and iPhone15 plus presents a huge leap foward with the dynamic island and spectacular camera upgrades,
while the iPhone15 Pro and iPhone15 Pro Max features solid and lightweight titanium design and A17 Pro for next-level performance and mobile gaming.


iPhone 15 has an upgraded 48MP Main Camera, a USB 2.0 Type C port, and the Dynamic Island.
You might be wandering, what's the difference between the iPhone15 and the iPhone15 Plus, there's not much of a difference just the
screen size and battery make the biggest differences. Both of these new models have a camera update that is taken from the Pro lineup of
2022 and are powered by the A16 chip from last year.
Additionally, they now have that funky pill-shaped cutout and the interesting Dynamic Island function. Apple iPhone15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max is also avilable.
Apple iPhone 15 will be availble on the market soon, pre-orders began on September 15, 2023.
